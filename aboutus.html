About Us

DONFORCE SECURITY GUARDS is a renowned, wholly indigenious, active, industrial, commercial security and risk management organization with head office in Lagos-Nigeria, West Africa, spreading to other states of the federation, with zonal offices in Abuja, Ibadan, Benin, Port-Harcourt, Lokoja, Kaduna, Kano and Maiduguri.

We are a corporate security organization fast growing in the industry with a solid capital base of over 800 million Naira with foreign technical partners from the United States of America. Our business is to train men and women to become professional security guards as in every organization security post is located at the front office, making the security officer the front office managers.

Our numerous clients speak about our track records and ability to provide quality man-power for security purposes.
